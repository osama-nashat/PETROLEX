Who Are We ?

We are the biggest company in the world

We help companies create and improve industry products and services through long lasting and mutually rewarding relationships.

Petrolex is designed for industrial, oil and gas, manufacturing, mechanical, power and energy, engineering & Multipurpose Business.

Petrolex is a leading international service organization focused on high pressure hoses and fluid connectors providing products and application engineering knowledge, as well as maintenance services, to all market segments through a global network tailored to local conditions.

Petrolex’s services are made available at the customer’s doorstep on 5 continents, through an ever-growing network of Hydroshops, On-site Workshops, and Service on-site Vans.
